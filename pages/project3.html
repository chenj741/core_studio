Userflow/Microinteraction

Community goodwill app that allows people in a neighborhood to provide and support each other during hard times. The app allows people to acquire things that they don't have access to, especially during quaratine.

What sets this app as a unique platform is the ability to merge requests between users as well as the time limits on interactions, making the interactions focused on action.

Wireflow/Microinteraction Sample

We mapped out a simple wireflow that shows how the user feeds, merging, and chat functions work.

A small validation animation was added to the onboarding to welcome the user. A thanks! animation was added to the page that appears at the end of a successful interaction to encourage optimism.

Microinteraction Prototypes

In order to emphasize the points of success in the app, I made a set of animated icons. These were made to feel energetic and upbeat.

The animations were then put into a lottie player to be played.

Working Prototype
