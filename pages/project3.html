Userflow/Microinteraction

Community goodwill app that allows people in a neighborhood to provide and support each other during hard times. The app allows people to acquire things that they don't have access to, especially during quaratine.

What sets this app as a unique platform is the ability to merge requests between users as well as the time limits on interactions, making the interactions focused on action. More microinteractions: When giving or receiving thanks, confetti pops out Location validation when joining a community Small timer for posts
