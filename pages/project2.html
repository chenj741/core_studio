Catalogue Content

For my catalogue content, I looked into Kangaroo words, which are words that contain their synonym with the same sequence of letters. I wanted to create an interactive website for a topic with multiple aspects, so kangaroo words were perfect. Also, I love kangaroos.

I put the 300+ known kangaroo words into a spreadsheet with their definitions. These were distinguished with a few filters. These included anti-kangaroo words- words that contain their antonyms. Both- words that contain both a synonym and an antonym. Second Joeys- words that contain two synonyms. and Grand Kangaroos- words that have a synonym within their synonym so that all three words are synonyms.
