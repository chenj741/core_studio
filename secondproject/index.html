Kangaroo Words

A Kangaroo is a fun classification for a word that contains its own synonym. The word nestled inside the Kangaroo is called a Joey.

There's much more to Kangaroo words than synonyms. Check out the Types of Kangaroos page to explore Anti-Kangaroos, Twin Joeys, Opposite Joeys, and Grand Kangaroos.
